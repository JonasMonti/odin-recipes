Francesinha

Description

The francesinha, in its special sandwich variation, is most commonly made up of sausage, fresh sausage, ham, cold cuts, and beef steak, covered with melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices Sandwich Bread
  • 4 slices Cheese
  • 2 slices Sandwich Ham
  • 1 Pork Sausage
  • 1 Smoked Pork Sausage
  • 1 Thin Steak
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • French Fries
  • Francesinha Sauce

Steps to make it

  1. Place a large skillet over medium to high heat. Add a tablespoon of olive oil and fry the sausages, turning them regularly, until evenly cooked. Cut them in half, then slice open lengthwise. Set aside.
  2. Use the residual oil in the skillet to cook the steak for about 2 minutes on each side or until golden brown. Season with salt and pepper. Set aside.
  3. Place a small non-stick pan over medium to high heat. Add one tablespoon of olive oil, then crack the egg in. Let it cook for 3 minutes uncovered for a runny yolk.
  4. While the egg cooks, lightly toast both slices of bread, then assemble the sandwich in the following order: bread, sliced ham, steak, sausages, bread.
  5. Bring the Francesinha sauce back to a boil.
  6. Place the fried egg on top of your sandwich, arrange the slices of cheese around it.
  7. Pour enough hot Francesinha sauce on top for it to melt the cheese and pool on the plate. Serve with French fries. Enjoy!
